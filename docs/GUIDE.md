The application provides four main AI-powered tools:

## 1. Smart Image Editor (Edit)
- **Function**: Modify image content based on a text description.
- **How to use**: Upload your image, then type the desired change (e.g., "Change the shirt color to blue" or "Add sunglasses").

## 2. Image Generator (Generate)
- **Function**: Convert text prompts into artistic images.
- **Advanced Options**: You can choose aspect ratios (16:9, 1:1), styles (Realistic, Anime), and even add a negative prompt for things you want to avoid.

## 3. Quality Enhancer (Enhance)
- **Function**: Upscale old or blurry images.
- **Modes**: 
  - **General**: Overall clarity improvement.
  - **Face**: Focuses on facial details.
  - **Denoise**: Removes digital grain and noise.

## 4. Background Remover (Remove BG)
- **Function**: Isolate the main subject and delete the background precisely.
- **Result**: You will receive a fully transparent PNG.

---
**Tip**: For best results, keep your prompts descriptive and clear in English or Arabic.
