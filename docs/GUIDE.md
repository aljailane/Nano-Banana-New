Harness the power of AI with our four specialized image processing tools:

## 1. Smart Image Editor
- **Capability**: Modifies specific elements of an image using natural language.
- **Instructions**: Upload a photo and describe the change (e.g., "Change background to a snowy forest" or "Give the person a red hat").

## 2. Image Generator
- **Capability**: Generates high-resolution images from scratch.
- **Pro Features**:
  - **Aspect Ratios**: 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, etc.
  - **Negative Prompts**: Define what the AI should avoid (e.g., "blurry, low quality").
  - **Styles**: Choose from Realistic, Anime, Oil Painting, and more.

## 3. Image Enhancer
- **Capability**: Restores and improves image quality.
- **Modes**: 
  - **General**: Sharpens and fixes lighting.
  - **Face**: Focused enhancement for portrait photography.
  - **Denoise**: Cleans up artifacts and grain.

## 4. Background Remover
- **Capability**: Semantic segmentation to isolate the subject.
- **Output**: Downloads as a high-quality transparent PNG.

---
**Tip**: Both English and Arabic prompts are supported. Clearer, more descriptive prompts yield superior results.
